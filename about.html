NOHrD designs follow the same blueprint set-out by WaterRower inventor John Duke. The aim of all NOHrD products is to offer beautiful, well-made equipment that does not compromise on either form or functionality. The first product released in the NOHrD range was the WallBars, which first came to market in the late 2000s. An immediate success, the WallBars were quickly followed by the NOHrD SwingBells and the WaterGrinder. The success of the first NOHrD product encouraged our team that there is a market of fitness enthusiast that do not want to compromise on performance or aesthetics when choosing their exercise equipment. The ever-growing range of NOHrD products has developed methodically to meet our customers requests and now include everything from the TriaTrainer, a folding workout bench, the Sprintbok, our award-winning curved treadmill.
